Methodology

Checklist

What We Test

  • Gross Margin > 40%Pricing power and strong unit economics.
  • SG&A Efficiency < 30%Overhead stays controlled as revenue grows.
  • R&D Reliance < 30%Healthy innovation without over‑spending.
  • Net Margin > 20%Meaningful profit after all costs.
  • Consistent EarningsProfitable in most years (5–10 based on filings).
  • Interest Coverage > 6xEarnings comfortably cover interest.
  • Debt / Equity < 0.5Conservative balance‑sheet leverage.
  • ROE > 15%Equity capital produces strong returns.
  • Capex Efficiency < 50%Cash generation not swallowed by capex.
  • $1 Test > 1.0Each $1 retained creates > $1 value.
Scoring

How the Score Works

Signals only count when data exists. Missing data shows as “No data” and does not count as a fail.

Score = passes / available signals. Example: 6/8 means two signals had no data.

Verdicts: Strong (8+), Mixed (5–7), Weak (<5).
